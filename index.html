About

Earthquake Engineering

Linear and Nonlinear Seismic Evaluation of a Reinforced Concrete Building

This project evaluates the seismic performance of an existing reinforced concrete building using linear and nonlinear analysis methods. STAAD.Pro was used for linear elastic and code-based design, while STERA 3D performed modal, pushover, and earthquake response analyses. The study compares code-based design with actual structural behavior, identifies potential failure mechanisms, and assesses the building's performance during earthquakes.
